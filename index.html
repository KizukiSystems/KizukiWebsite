Bridging the Gap Between Infrastructure and Innovation.

Kizuki Systems is a property technology and consulting firm founded by Tetsuro Ide. We specialize in transforming fragmented residential environments into cohesive, highly efficient systems.

Drawing on a foundational background studying Engineering Physics and Integrated Science at Carleton University, combined with extensive, hands-on experience in building maintenance and community leadership, Kizuki Systems builds solutions that work in the real world—not just on paper.

Core Philosophy

  • Centralization: Unifying disjointed smart devices and building systems into single control interfaces.
  • Practicality: Leveraging tangible building maintenance experience to design tech that actually solves operational friction.
  • Community: Building tools that empower resident associations, property managers, and homeowners alike.
